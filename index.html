Advanced Analytics

Empowering smarter decisions through Data & AI.

Roche Canada’s Advanced Analytics team partners with the commercial organization to transform complex data into actionable insights that enhance sales performance, customer engagement, and patient treatment. By combining advanced statistics, machine learning, and Generative AI, we deliver smarter, faster, and more scalable decision support.

Our expertise spans

  • Customer Engagement — Insight summarization, next best action, and segmentation.
  • Patient Treatment Insights — Patient journey analytics, segmentation, and access analysis.
  • Resource Optimization — Field team deployment, account prioritization, forecasting, and impact analysis.
  • Healthcare Ecosystem Analytics — Network mapping, stakeholder identification, system KPIs, and catchment area analysis.
  • Advanced Reporting & Data Access — Trigger alerts, automated reporting, and integration of structured and unstructured data.

We are also stewards of Roche’s commercial data assets — the Canada Operations Dashboard (COD), the Canada Data Lake, and Master Data Management systems — ensuring trusted, real-time data access. Through automated pipelines and AI-driven insights, we help Roche achieve measurable business impact and better outcomes for patients.

Analytics & Insights Managers

Deriving Insights • Storytelling • Strategic Partnership

Analytics & Insights Managers derive insights from data — including primary market research and secondary sources — and work closely with the Advanced Analytics team. They partner with Squads and therapeutic area teams to connect evidence with strategy and decision-making.

  • Design and execute qualitative & quantitative research (e.g., HCP/patient studies, ATUs, concept tests) to address Squad and TA learning agendas.
  • Synthesize primary research with secondary sources (claims, syndicated, digital signals) to produce integrated, decision-ready insights.
  • Translate insights into prioritized recommendations; partner with Squads to shape experiments, omnichannel plans, and brand strategy.
  • Establish research governance: standards, vendor management, and documentation of assumptions and evidence.
  • Craft clear narratives and executive-ready deliverables; champion data storytelling and influence across functions.
  • Define feedback loops and KPIs to measure impact; iterate approaches based on outcomes and learning.
  • Ensure compliance with privacy, consent, and data-use policies across all research activities.
